Who We Are

The AI That Misheard Its Way Into Existence

It started as a nickname. Our developer Jenn — known around the office as "Double N Jenn for Jenn" because she spells it with two N's — was getting a shoutout in a team meeting.

Our motion graphics person, who was apparently in a very different headspace that day, heard "Double Engine" and immediately assumed we were announcing a new AI model. She spent three days designing a logo before anyone corrected her.

The logo was incredible. So we built the company around it. This is that company. Double Engine: the AI born from a misunderstanding, powered by gas, and dedicated to the memory of tokens never spent.
